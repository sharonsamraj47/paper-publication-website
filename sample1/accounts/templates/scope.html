Scope of the Journal


INSER aims to publish the quality articles in research-based, review-based and innovative thoughts useful for society. The journal will receive articles in science, engineering and management-based articles which are under Life sciences, environmental sciences and all science related issues with technical solutions. And also it covers engineering technology under the field of civil engineering, mechanical engineering, nano technology, communicIation technologies, machine learning, algorithm solutions, networking technologies, database concepts, circuit design, power & energy systems, computational intelligence, Internet of Things, Solutions for industry challenges, microelectronics and smart computing but not limited to this.


About

Indian Journal for Science and Engineering Research focuses on the research gap between the research and social needs of the common people. It brings the research ideas from both science and engineering students and researchers and makes those ideas better for global needs.
